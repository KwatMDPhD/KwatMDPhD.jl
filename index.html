I'm a sixth-year MD-PhD candidate at the Stanford School of Medicine.

My life's task is to build the everything blood test 🩸🔮

I’m building a blood test in the Stanford Mark Davis Lab. I’ve been coding analytics that use a causality model to analyze various cells and molecules. The data can be generated from a standard blood draw, but getting one can still be cumbersome for many people. So, I’m developing a platform that allows for blood collection and data generation from anyone, anywhere, at any time, seamlessly.

I continuously sharpen the platform and the causality analytics, simultaneously providing value to others. I assist many scientists, clinicians, and companies in generating data and analyzing it to meet their specific project needs. I’ve also launched a non-clinical version of this test to help as many people as possible gain useful information from their blood.
